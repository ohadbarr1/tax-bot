# Tax Data Changelog

## 2024-01-01 — Initial data entry (P3)

### Files updated
- `tax_brackets_2024_2025.json` — Progressive tax brackets for 2024 and 2025
- `credit_points_2024.json` — Credit point rules for 2024
- `credit_points_2025.json` — Credit point rules for 2025
- `periphery_postcodes.json` — Periphery-eligible postcodes (ITA Section 11)
- `ita_form_urls.json` — Official ITA form URLs

### Sources consulted
- ITA (רשות המסים) official publications for tax year 2024: https://www.gov.il/he/departments/israel_tax_authority
- Bank of Israel Annual Interest Rate for 2024 (BoI): ₪3.71/USD
- ITA credit point values: ₪2,904/year (2024), ₪3,000/year (2025)
- Periphery classification: National Periphery and Frontier Development Authority
- Section 11 periphery tax benefits: ITA circular 2024

### Notes
- Disability credit points per Section 9(5) of Income Tax Ordinance
- Oleh Chadash credit points per Section 35 of Income Tax Ordinance
- Soldier discharge credit per Section 35A of Income Tax Ordinance
- All bracket thresholds are annual (not monthly)
- Credit point value is annual (monthly × 12)
